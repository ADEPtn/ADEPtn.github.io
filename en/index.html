About Us

Founded in Tunisia in 2017, ADEP is a non-governmental organization (NGO) whose main mission is to contribute to the environmental, social, and economic sustainability of communities.

We are strategically positioned to promote best practices and communicate international standards, emphasizing shared responsibilities in protecting our precious environmental and cultural heritage, especially in the face of global changes.
